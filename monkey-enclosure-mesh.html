Monkey Enclosure Mesh & Cage Protection

Flexible, high-tensile stainless steel wire rope mesh designed for active primates, offering safety, visibility, and durability.

Stainless steel wire rope mesh for Monkey Enclosure Mesh

Stainless steel rope mesh is specially designed for monkey enclosure mesh, from AISI 304/316 stainless steel cables, hand-woven. It is often used as monkey enclosure mesh and large monkey cage mesh, providing clear sight for tourists to watch monkeys and enhance enjoyment.

Our stainless steel mesh fence for monkeys has the following features:

  • Safety-certified: Extreme toughness, capable of loading a strong force.
  • Excellent elasticity: High rigidity, adapting perfectly to the habit of monkey climbing.
  • Good perspective: Providing excellent vision for visitors.
  • Low maintenance: Easy to clean, environmentally friendly, and cost-effective.
  • Durability: Simple structure, strong, and built to last over 30 years.

Stainless steel wire rope mesh is professional monkey enclosure mesh, suitable for a variety of monkey varieties, such as golden monkey, lemurs, langurs, macaques, capuchin monkeys, long-tail monkeys, and rhinopithecus.

Monkey's tunnels are very popular in more and more zoos and exhibition designs. Tunnel mesh creates more activity space and fun for the monkeys, providing connecting passageways between habitats.

Specifications For Monkey enclosure

In general, the size of monkey determines the size of mesh aperture, the degree of monkey's climbing habit determines the rope strength, and specifications are selected according to the actual exhibit design.

Rope diameter, material, and mesh aperture size can all be customized. We use SS304/316 stainless steel with structures ranging from 7*7 to 7*19 cores depending on the diameter (1.6mm to 3.2mm). The wire rope is smooth, ensuring it won't damage the primate's fur while being tough enough for their active climbing.

We provide the regular specifications for your reference.
