About Unique Wire Netting

Your Trusted Partner in Industrial & Architectural Mesh Solutions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide world-class wire mesh solutions, Unique Wire Netting has grown into a leading manufacturer and exporter in the industry. We specialize in stainless steel cable mesh, welded wire mesh, and custom netting solutions for a diverse range of applications including zoos, aviaries, architectural facades, and industrial filtration.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We utilize high-grade materials such as SS304 and SS316 to ensure superior corrosion resistance and longevity. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped to handle both large-scale production and custom fabrication needs.

Our Core Values
