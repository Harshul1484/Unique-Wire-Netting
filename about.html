About Unique Wire Netting

Building Strength. Defining Flexibility. Since 2008.

Executive Summary

Unique Wire Netting is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-quality wire mesh solutions. Established in 2008, we have dedicated over 15 years to mastering the art of wire weaving and fabrication. From intricate architectural designs to heavy-duty industrial security, our products serve as the backbone for diverse sectors across India.

We specialize in high-grade Stainless Steel Cable Wire Mesh, offering both Ferrule and Hand-Woven types, alongside a comprehensive catalog of industrial and decorative meshes.

Our Core Product Portfolio

We provide a versatile range of products engineered for durability and precision:

A. Stainless Steel Cable Mesh

  • Ferrule Type: High-strength mesh held together by seamless ferrules. Ideal for safety balustrades, bridge fencing, and modern architectural accents.
  • Hand-Woven Type: A flexible, "interwoven" mesh that offers high transparency and tensile strength. It is the preferred choice for zoo enclosures, aviary nets, and green walls.

B. Industrial Mesh Solutions

  • Welded Wiremesh: Uniform grid patterns with high stability, used in construction, machine guarding, and agricultural fencing.
  • Chainlink Wiremesh: Durable, corrosion-resistant fencing for boundaries, parks, and high-security zones.
  • Carbon Steel Wire Mesh: Designed for rugged industrial applications requiring high impact resistance and structural integrity.

C. Specialty & Design Mesh

Decorative Wiremesh: A fusion of aesthetics and function. Used for interior partitions, ceiling panels, and furniture design to provide a sophisticated metallic texture.
